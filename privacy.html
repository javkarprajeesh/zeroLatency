HOW WE COLLECT YOUR INFORMATION

We generally collect or receive your information in three ways:

  1. When you voluntarily provide information - such as when making a booking, create your player profile, or sign up for email updates;
  2. Automatic recording of your information - such as through cookies or our games; and
  3. Information collected from third parties - analytics and advertising third parties may provide your information to assist in marketing activities.

Please noteWe generally only ask you for your personal information to provide the services you request. You can always choose to not provide the information, and also request we delete your information, however in some instances this will mean that we are not able to provide the services you have requested, such as booking or playing a game.

  1. Information you provide

Your information is collected when you enter or otherwise provide it as per the below. The information we collect may change, as the services and features offered change over time, however the following are examples of what information you may provide to us:

  • Your name, email, language, and phone number (when making a booking)
  • In addition to the above, your player display name, date of birth, dominant hand, height, and gender (when creating a player account to play our games)
  • Your feedback and ratings (when completing a survey)
  • Your enquiries or support requests, including your message and account or order details (when you contact us with an enquiry or request help)

You don’t have to share this information with us, however it may be required in order to book or play our FRVR games.

Please note, when you play a game some of your information will be publicly accessible to others, such as your player display name and your game performance statistics (Such as on the public scoreboard at the end of a game within the facility where you played only).

  1. Automatic recording of your information

Some information is collected automatically when you visit our website or play our games, such as from web browsers, cookies, log files, and related technology. The information collected is typically aggregated and used as statistical data to determine trends among a group of players, instead of being used to identify individuals. Examples include:

  • The site location of your booking.
  • The location of your device, such as from your IP address.
  • Website behavior information, such the website pages you've visited, which website you came from before or went to after, and what device you were using.
  • Game play information, such as the games you have played, when you have played, how many times you have played, and your game performance statistics.
  • Error reports, such as if your equipment fails, or your game experiences and issue.
  1. Receive information from third parties

In some instances, your information is received and collected from third parties.

Examples include:

  • Third parties who provide advertising (Facebook Business or Google Adwords) and analytics tools (Such as from Google Analytics), may provide us with your information to supporting our marketing activities, advertising campaigns, and the improvement of services.
