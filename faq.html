What should I wear?

This is an active experience so please make sure you wear comfortable clothes and appropriate footwear (i.e. no high heels). We also recommend not to wear mascara as it can smudge the lens and impact your experience.

Can I Wear my glasses?

With our Gen 2 technology 99% of glasses fit with the headset lenses (unless it is odd sized).

Our headsets have adjustment tabs under each lens so that players who are not able to fit glasses can adjust the focus of the headsets to compensate for approximately +1 to -3.8 diopters.

Who can or can't play?

Players must be 13 years of age (players under 18 are advised to play with parental supervision) or above to play.

Players under the influence of alcohol or drugs will not be allowed to play.

You must be able to walk around unassisted for an hour with full use of both hands. People with one eye vision are able to play.

For safety reasons anyone with the following impairment/medical conditions are not allowed to play:

  • Epilepsy and similar seizure disorders.
  • Deaf or severe hearing loss.
  • Broken leg or arm.
  • Wheelchair users.

Our experiences are not recommended for anyone with the following impairment/medical conditions:

  • Heart condition.
  • Heavily pregnant.

Can this experience cause motion sickness?

Every part of the system is designed to give you an immersive experience without any of the motion sickness typically associated with virtual reality systems.

Will I walk into a wall?

No. We have in-game safety mechanisms and a Game Master to prevent that to happen.

How big a space can I move in?

You will have approximately 2000 sq. feet of game space, but the virtual space is much larger. We reuse the space with some nifty tricks that we have developed.
